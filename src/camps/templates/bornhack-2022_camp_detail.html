BornHack is a 7 day outdoor tent camp where hackers, makers and people with an interest in technology or security come together to celebrate technology, socialise, learn and have fun.
{% thumbnail 'img/bornhack-2020' 'logotent_pink_people.jpg' 'BornHack Logotent with pink light and people' %}
{% thumbnail 'img/bornhack-2020' 'participant_tent_laptop.jpg' 'hacker with laptop in front of tent' %}
BornHack 2022 was the seventh BornHack. It took place from Wednesday the 3rd of August to Wednesday the 10th of August 2022 at our venue on the Danish island of Funen.

The BornHack team is very grateful for all the volunteers who helped make BornHack 2022 a success. Their names will stay visible in our People page as a permanent token of our gratitude.
{% thumbnail 'img/bornhack-2021' 'volunteers.jpg' 'The BornHack 2021 volunteers' %}

{% thumbnail 'img/bornhack-2018/fonsmark' 'FB1_9970_cropped.JPG' 'Danish politicians, government officals and IT-developers debating at BornHack 2018' %}
BornHack 2022 had a great lineup of hackers, makers, politicians, activists, developers, artists, sysadmins, engineers with something to say on the program. Most of the content was recorded and is availble on video.

BornHack aims to keep ticket prices affordable for everyone and to that end we had sponsors for BornHack 2022. Please see our sponsors page to see who the BornHack 2022 sponsors were.
